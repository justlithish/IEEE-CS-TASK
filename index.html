Form factor

Wearable wrist device — lightweight aluminum, IP68 water resistance.

Display

1.4" AMOLED touchscreen with adaptive brightness and always-on mode.

AI features

On-device speech recognition, cloud personalization, mood tracking, secure local storage.

Audio & Connectivity

Bone-conduction speaker, haptic feedback, Bluetooth 5.2, Wi‑Fi, optional LTE.

Battery

Up to 2 days typical use; 7 days on low-power mode. Fast USB-C charging.

How does this watch work?

  1. 1
    Set up:

    Pair with the companion app and choose preferences.

  2. 2
    Listen & Sense:

    The device monitors prompts and scheduled check-ins locally.

  3. 3
    Conversation:

    AI responds with supportive dialogue and suggested actions.

  4. 4
    Personalization:

    Optional anonymized sync improves personalization over time.

  5. 5
    Review & act:

    Use the app to review summaries, insights, and adjust settings.

Note: Companion provides supportive conversations but is not a replacement for professional mental health care.
